About Our School

Discover the legacy of Ganesh P.S.

Welcome to Ganesh P.S.

Established in 2005, Ganesh P.S. has been providing quality education to the children of Kritpura and surrounding areas for over 20 years. Our mission is to create a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth.

We believe in the all-round development of our students through a balanced approach to academics, sports, arts, and moral education.
